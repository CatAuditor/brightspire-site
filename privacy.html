Privacy Policy

Brightspire LLC  ·  Effective Date: April 7, 2025  ·  Applies to: Sortie Field (iOS)

Brightspire LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Sortie Field mobile application. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user. Sortie Field is intended for use in the United States.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. When you sign in via Google, we receive your name and email address. Your display name is stored in our system to identify you to your campaign coordinators and teammates.
  • Precise location. While you are actively using the app, we collect your GPS coordinates to show your position on the campaign map and share it with other active volunteers on your team. Location is collected only while the app is in the foreground.
  • Canvassing activity. Contact logs you create (door status, notes, timestamps, household ID) are stored and associated with your account.
  • App usage data. We store records of actions taken within the app (e.g., contacts logged, pickups created) to power campaign reporting and coordinator dashboards.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the app and display campaign data to you and your team.
  • To share your real-time location with other volunteers on the same campaign while you are active.
  • To generate campaign statistics and reports for coordinators.
  • To manage team membership, roles, and invite-based access.

We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

  • Campaign coordinators. Coordinators in your campaign can see your display name, contact logs, and general activity within that campaign.
  • Teammates. Other volunteers on your campaign can see your real-time location on the map while you are both active.
  • Supabase. Our database and authentication infrastructure is hosted by Supabase, Inc. Data is stored on servers in the United States. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf and does not use your data independently.

4. Data Retention

Your account data and contact logs are retained for the duration of the campaign and for a reasonable period afterward for reporting purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the address below.

5. Security

Access to your data is controlled by row-level security policies — each user can only access data for campaigns they are a member of. All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.

6. Children's Privacy

Sortie Field is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
